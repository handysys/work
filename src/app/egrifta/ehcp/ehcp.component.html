PROGRAM SERVICES
Access and assistance throughout treatment
Expert Patient Care Coordinators help patients:
  • Enroll in the EGRIFTA® Co-Pay Assistance Program that covers up to $6,000 per calendar year, if eligible
    • Only for commercially insured patients
    • Maximum of 12 prescriptions per year
    • No activation required by you or your physician
  • Identify the best insurance and coverage options
  • Follow up about your insurance benefits
  • Navigate the prior authorization (PA) processes for insurance companies
  • Find in-network specialty pharmacies
  • Prepare for therapy (i.e. educational materials and sharps containers, if needed)
  • Verify eligibility to enroll in the EGRIFTA® Financial Assistance Program that may help with the cost of therapy
